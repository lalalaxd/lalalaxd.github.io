Types Of Cancer

Lung Cancer

When cancer starts in the lungs, it is called lung cancer. Lung cancers usually are grouped into two main types called small cell and non-small cell.

Bladder Cancer

The bladder is a balloon-shaped organ in your lower abdomen, near the pelvis. It stores urine from the kidneys until it is passed out of the body.

Kidney Cancer

The body has two kidneys, one on each side of the body, located behind the liver and stomach.

Who We Are

Tales of Cancer Warriors is a dedicated platform created to provide support, information, and inspiration to individuals and families affected by cancer. Our mission is to offer a beacon of hope through shared stories, reliable resources, and a community that understands the journey of battling cancer.

Founded by survivors and caregivers, our foundation aims to bridge the gap between diagnosis and recovery by providing comprehensive information about different types of cancer, treatment options, and coping strategies. We believe in the power of storytelling and the positive impact it can have on those fighting cancer and their loved ones.
